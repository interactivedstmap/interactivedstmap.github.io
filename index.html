DST is now the dominant time in the US. It is no longer solely a summertime experience. It exists for 65% of the year.

Decreased life expectancy, shortened sleep, increased body weight, breast cancer, diabetes, heart disease, sleep disturbances, and mental & cognitive problems have been attributed to it. None of this was even imagined when DSTs were initially created.

As to serious science, the 2017 Nobel Prize for Physiology/ Medicine was awarded for discoveries of mechanisms that control circadian rhythms. More than 50% of all gene expression is under circadian control — a massive influence on physiology.

After using this interactive map, you may find that a "Western Edge Effect" becomes apparent.
